About Our Project

Thanks for stopping by! This project is a collaboration between Fiona and Vivian. We both conducted separate research for the topic of diversity and came together in hopes of creating an interactive platform that that could help bring awareness and connect designers of color together. Through our collaboration, we implemented the Designers of Color Map to show designers how diverse the Design discipline is and to have a sense of pride in your own contributions as a designer of color.
